Steganography: The Art and Science of Hiding Things in Other Things

Link to full Blog Post

This series of blog posts introduces the reader to steganography, the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. It then builds on that foundation to introduce error correcting codes - specifically, Hamming codes - and some of the math behind it all.

The sample code which formed the nucleus for this series was part of a class project for my discrete math class in college, and can be found on Github.

All of this content can also be found on the Striker Security Blog.
